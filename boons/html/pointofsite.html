The boons calculator is a flexible tool for calculating any taskpoint or boon related issues. Based on your input, it can base its calculations on money you wish to donate, taskpoints or boons to be gained etc. It also has an option to calculate half boons after certain full boon, which usually is BAD, but may be useful in some combos. It even tries to use the remaining taskpoints to smaller boons until nothing can be get anymore. It even shows how many taskpoints are left after the boons!
