Welcome!

Welcome to my new site. I hope this site will be the one of the most useful BatMUD related sites with unique tools and information you can't get elsewhere. The point of this site is to gather my previously very fragmented tools under a single site with common interface to them all.
Note: I made a major change (18th Feb 2004) in way this site replies to your requests. All data is now gzipped (for you whose browser says it understands gzipped stuff). It makes pageloading HUGELY faster, ie Reinc Simulator reply goes down from 160kB to 10kB, and (new) Batmap goes down from 850kB to 35kB!
