Hp:0/10 Sp:0/10 Ep:0/10 >In here you can advance your level. Most of your stats will rise with your
level. The levels you advance here will allow you to advance in your guilds.
Some stairs lead upward to the Top Players room. Special bonuses can be gotten
from the Boon room downstairs. An information center for quests can be found
to the south.
The following commands can be used here:
   advance        : to advance a level
   advance block  : invest experience into the next level
   cost           : tells you how much exp is needed for your next level
   train <stat>   : train <stat> with your training points
   train all      : train evenly all your stats using all training points
   tcost <stat>   : training points needed from current <stat> to <value>
   list           : to list levels and experience costs
   total <lvl>    : total cost from level 1 to <lvl>
Obvious exits are: n, s, u, d and e.
