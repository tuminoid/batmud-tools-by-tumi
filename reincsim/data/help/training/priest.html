Evil Priests

Command: priests
Creator: coren
Maximum level: 35

Description:
Evil Priests originally started as a small cult with few devoted followers,
but over the years its strength, as well as the number of followers increased.
Their hatred grew with every new member. They chose to follow several evil
gods instead of being singularly devoted to any one specificly. But they are
closest to Burglefloogah and Draen-Dalar in temperment. Draen-Dalar accepts
any mortal who wishes to follow him, but his pledges must make a life time
commitment to serve only evil. For this reason initiates to the guild start
out serving only Draen-Dalar. Only after they obtain his blessings by
obtaining power and serving evil in its truest form can they expand their
horizons and commit themselves to other gods for additional power. Evil is a
general thing and everyone has a bit within them. The stronger the evil the
better and the more powerful the priest. The High Priest is the gods' main
link to the world therefore more powerful and has greater responsibility
within the guild itself.

Both gods smile upon those who use their evil gifts to bring evil into the
world, although it has been rumored that the harming of a newbie can have dire
consequences. For a newbie's path is uncertain that soon in life, so little,
if any, should be done to harm or hinder them. One should also strive to be
evil incarnate and avoid those that are pure of heart. For a time of reckoning
will come. And then the follower will truly know how they appear in their gods
eyes...
