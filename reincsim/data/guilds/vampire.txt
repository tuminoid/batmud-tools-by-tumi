                          The Princes of Darkness

   Command: princes_of_darkness
   Creator: [22]Kato
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   The Princes of Darkness are the powerful masters of the night, they
   stalk this
   realm gathering wisdom and power for their brethren, the knowledge of
   which
   they share via a sacred book kept in safe keeping at the shrine of the
   Prince
   of Darkness.
