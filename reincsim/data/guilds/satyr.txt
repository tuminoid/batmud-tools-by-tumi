                         The Revelers of the Night

   Command: revelers
   Creator: [22]Dragoness
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   The Revelers of the Night is a right of passage for all young satyrs.
   They
   must party seven days and nights in row, before being accepted as a
   'grown up'
   satyr. For this reason, satyrs have received reputation as incredible
   party
   animals.
