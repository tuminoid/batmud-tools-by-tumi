                          The Wisps of the Forest

   Command: the_wisps
   Creator: [22]Sirdar
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   Sprites have been in the world as compatriots with the elves, created
   by gods
   to entertain the eternal elves, who felt lonely. Sprites share all
   with the
   elves, the mindset, appearance, culture and values, but not size.
   Sprites were
   created to be small, not meant to burden to this world. The sprites,
   now,
   revere their 'mother', who was the first female sprite created by gods
   to roam
   the world.
