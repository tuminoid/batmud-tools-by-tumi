                        The Maosoleum Defense Corps

   Command: corpse
   Creator: [22]Dragoness
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   Maosoleum Defense Corps, or the MDC, is defense organization of
   Maosoleum,
   which is the greatest Skeleton city in the world. The Maosoleum
   Defense Corps
   was formed to offer some basic training for Maosoleum citizens, so
   that in
   case of an attack, the citizens can defend their fair city. MDC also
   teaches
   it's citizens some valuable spells, which may help them serve the
   Maosoleum
   better.
