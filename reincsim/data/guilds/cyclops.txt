                      School of Survival for one-eyes

   Command: one-eye
   Creator: [22]Dragoness
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   The school of survival is a school for young cyclops to learn ways
   with which
   to feed themselves in the cruel world. They learn to fish, hunt and to
   sleep
   well after having a meal. They also learn to bash sheep skulls in so
   that they
   can eat their delicacy.
