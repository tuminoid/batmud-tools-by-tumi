                         The Collegium of Inventors

   Command: inventor
   Creators: [22]Hablo, [23]Shardik
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   Many races have inviduals that are specialized in creating novel
   concepts and
   invetions. But the most known inventors almost excluvisvely gnomish.
   This is
   no wonder, considering the innovative and active minds of these small
   people.
   Gnomes have even formed the Collegium of Inventors, a guild that
   guides young
   gnomes on refining their ideas into full blown machines and gadgets.
   Sometimes
   the laws of physics are in the way of their inventions, therefore
   little magic
   is required to make things work.
