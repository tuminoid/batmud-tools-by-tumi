                            Soldiers of Oblivion

   Command: soldiers_of_oblivion
   Creator: [22]Zoso
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   The Soldiers of Oblivion are the powerful shadows that have formed a
   group in
   order to survive better in the physical realm. These Soldiers are very
   determined and powerful, gaining more understanding of the physical
   realm
   every day. The aim for racial supremacy is one of the goals of the
   guild, as
   they seek to gain power over the physical like they already have the
   power
   over the shadow essence.
