                             The Orcish Raiders

   Command: raiders
   Creator: [22]Shinarae
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   The race of orcs are a warlike people who spend most of their time in
   underground caves. However they do come out onto the surface, where
   they are
   at best tolerated, for orcs are famous for attacking travellers,
   settlements,
   and even cities with little to no provocation. While not every orc is
   such an
   opportunistic rogue, just about every orc has learned, formally or
   otherwise,
   the basic principles of combat used in raiding parties.
