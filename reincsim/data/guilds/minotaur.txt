                        The Order of the Raging Bull

   Command: bull_order
   Creator: [22]Hablo
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   It is no wonder why minotaurs are feared and raging minotaurs spread
   terror to
   their enemies and friends alike. Every young minotaur spends a part of
   their
   youth under the guidance of The Order of the Raging Bull, the church
   of the
   great bull god Taurus. The Order teaches young minotaurs to project
   their
   anger in combat in most effective way against the enemy.
