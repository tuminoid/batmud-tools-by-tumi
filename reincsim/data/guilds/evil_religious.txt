                         Evil Religious background

   Command: evil_religious
   Creators: [22]Alaron, [23]Ikar, [24]Shardik
   Maximum level: 10
   Description:
   Starting at a young age, one is taught the different paths in life
   that
   can be chosen. At some point, a choice is made, for better or for
   worse.
   Those that chose to follow a life of evil grow in the power of causing
   harm to others, both through magic and strength.
