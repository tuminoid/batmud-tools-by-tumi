                             Magical background

   Command: magical
   Creators: [22]Durand, [23]Shardik
   Maximum level: 10
   Description:
   The magical background indicates that your parents are from a magical
   culture. The skills and spells that you learn during the first 10
   levels
   symbolize things what you would learn from your parents. You can't
   choose
   what you are taught.
