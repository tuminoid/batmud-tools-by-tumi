                         Good Religious background

   Command: good_religious
   Creators: [22]Alaron, [23]Ikar, [24]Shardik
   Maximum level: 10
   Description:
   Starting at a young age, one is taught the different paths in life
   that
   can be chosen. At some point, a choice is made, for better or for
   worse.
   Those that choose to follow the path of light will grow in the powers
   of healing and of helping others.
