                          The Warrior Brotherhood

   Command: monk
   Creator: [22]Delecti
   Maximum level: 30
   Description:
   The Warrior Brotherhood is a group of people who have decided to
   abandon
   the trivialities of society and live in a closed environment, the
   monestary.
   They live by a very strict code of honor and have taken a vow of
   poverty.
   The members of the brotherhood only use their skills in self-defence
   and
   believe that using weapons shows unforgivable weakness and despise
   everyone
   who does so.
