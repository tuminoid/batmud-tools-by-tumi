                             Shirrifs of Shire

   Command: shirrif
   Creator: [22]Walin
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   The shirrifs are responsible for the peace and quiet of the Shire.
   They are
   also sworn to protect and help (this used to be serve, but after
   numerous
   misunderstandings it was changed) any hobbit in need, and to apprehend
   evil
   doers.
