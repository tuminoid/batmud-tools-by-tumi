                            Citizens of the Pond

   Command: duck_pond
   Creator: [22]Dragoness
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   Citizens of the Pond is a loosely organized guild for ducks. Ducks
   teach each
   other how to fish and swim, and how to hear when dangerous people
   approach, so
   that they can hide from them.

                                New Member
                    Receive bonuses as you advance
                    May train skill [23]Fishing to 5%
                    May train skill [24]Alertness to 5%
                    May train skill [25]Hiding to 5%
                    May study spell [26]Call pigeon to 5%

                                  Level 2
                    May train skill [27]Fishing to 28%
                    May train skill [28]Alertness to 16%
                    May train skill [29]Hiding to 16%
                    May study spell [30]Call pigeon to 16%

                                  Level 3
                    May train skill [31]Fishing to 52%
                    May train skill [32]Alertness to 28%
                    May train skill [33]Hiding to 27%
                    May study spell [34]Call pigeon to 27%

                                  Level 4
                    May train skill [35]Fishing to 76%
                    May train skill [36]Alertness to 40%
                    May train skill [37]Hiding to 38%
                    May study spell [38]Call pigeon to 38%

                                  Level 5
                    May train skill [39]Fishing to 100%
                    May train skill [40]Hiding to 50%
                    May study spell [41]Call pigeon to 50%

                   Allows joining to the following guild:
                     Citizens of the Pond
