                         Denizens of the Underdark

   Command: denizens
   Creator: [22]Tedra
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   Drow religion is a subject of much mystery and legend. The most
   popular of
   religion is the chaotic worship of the goddess Lloth. Lloth happens to
   be the
   most unmerciful god that the drow worship. Drow that worship Lloth
   will more
   than likely be as forgiving of intrusion as their deity. Even though
   not all
   Drow worship this mean spirited goddess, they all strive to stay in
   her good
   graces.
