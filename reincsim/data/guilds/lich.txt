   Creator: [22]Dragoness
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   The liches have their own secret school of magic. This school is
   called the
   Shadow of Maosoleum. In this school, the newly reborn liches learn
   dark
   secrets which they use to rule the city of Maosoleum.
