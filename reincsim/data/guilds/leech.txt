                        Blood Brothers with Attitude

   Command: bloodbrothers
   Creator: [22]Malitrius
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   The Brotherhood of Blood or blood Brothers with attitude. Two of many
   names on
   this famous society of leeches. Here all young leeches learn how to
   control
   their great mental power under Hirudinean's supervision. But they also
   learn
   about blood and bloods importance for the leech race. The obligations
   of a
   blood brother to the leech race are also taught here.

                                New Member
                 Receive bonuses as you advance
                 May study spell [23]Energy drain to 10%
                 May study spell [24]Life leech to 15%
                 May study spell [25]Cure light wounds to 5%

                                  Level 2
                 May study spell [26]Energy drain to 20%
                 May study spell [27]Life leech to 23%
                 May study spell [28]Cure light wounds to 12%

                                  Level 3
                 May study spell [29]Energy drain to 30%
                 May study spell [30]Life leech to 32%
                 May study spell [31]Cure light wounds to 20%
                 May study spell [32]Mind blast to 5%

                                  Level 4
                 May study spell [33]Energy drain to 40%
                 May study spell [34]Life leech to 41%
                 May study spell [35]Cure light wounds to 27%
                 May study spell [36]Mind blast to 32%

                                  Level 5
                 May study spell [37]Energy drain to 50%
                 May study spell [38]Life leech to 50%
                 May study spell [39]Cure light wounds to 35%
                 May study spell [40]Mind blast to 60%
