                             The Royal Warriors

   Command: royal_warriors
   Creator: [22]Shardik
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   The titans are, despite their size, very intelligent and wise folk.
   Unable to
   be somewhat arrogant (after all, they ARE one of the very best), they
   mask
   their feelings by remaining silent while the lesser beings chatter
   away. Their
   guild reflects this attitude, located on top of an isolated mountain.
   Here the
   titans learn the warrior's skills that they soon perfect.
