                             Eternal Guardians

   Command: eternal_guardians
   Creator: [22]Zoso
   Maximum level: 5
   Description:
   The gargoyles were made for a single purpose: to be perfect guardians.
   In
   their sacred race shrine they train the important skills needed to
   guard with
   maximum efficiency. It is said that not even the end of the world
   would
   distract a gargoyle on guard duty.
